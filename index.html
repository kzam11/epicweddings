What We Do

Everything your wedding
day deserves

From the first song of your ceremony to the last dance of the night — we cover every moment.

Wedding DJ

Club-trained DJs with seamless mixing and zero dead air. We read the room, build energy and keep every generation dancing.

MC & Hosting

Warm, professional and effortlessly confident. Bernard guides your night from introductions to last dance — no cheese, no cringe.

Live Music

Soloists, duos, trios and full bands — perfectly matched to your vibe. We coordinate everything so you don't have to.

Production Lighting

Dance floor visuals and ambient lighting design that transforms any venue. From intimate glow to full production spectacle.

Ceremony Music

Set the perfect tone for your vows — live soloist, curated playlist or both. Seamless audio for every ceremony moment.

Cocktail Hour

The transition that sets the mood for everything that follows. Curated background music while your guests mingle and glasses clink.
