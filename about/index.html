Meet Bernard

The DJ who turns your wedding into the party your guests never forget.

"I don't just show up and press play. Every set is a conversation with your crowd."

Bernard Zamiara

Owner & Head DJ

20+ years.
Thousands of dancefloors.

Bernard Zamiara started behind the decks at Melbourne's club scene — not at weddings — and that makes all the difference.

Where most wedding DJs follow a formula, Bernard reads the room. He's spent two decades learning how crowds move, what makes people stay on the dancefloor, and how to build an arc across an entire night. That expertise now goes directly into every wedding he performs at.

As both DJ and MC, Bernard manages the entire entertainment experience — from your ceremony walk-in to the last song of the night. He works with you beforehand to understand your style, your must-plays, and the moments that matter most. On the night, he executes flawlessly and adapts in real time to whatever the crowd needs.

Beyond weddings, Bernard hosts the Epic House Party — a weekly radio show broadcast on Tune1 (Fridays, 5pm AWST), LiquidRadio (Fridays, 9:30pm AEST) and OzzMixx (Saturdays, 11pm AEST) with over 22,000 listeners worldwide. It's this ongoing connection to current music that keeps his sets fresh and relevant.

He's proudly inclusive — committed to celebrating every couple, every culture and every kind of love. If you're getting married in Victoria or border NSW, Bernard would love to be part of your day.
