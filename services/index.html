Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you travel outside Melbourne? +

Yes — we service all of Victoria and border NSW regions, including Albury-Wodonga, the Yarra Valley, Mornington Peninsula, Geelong, Ballarat and beyond. Travel fees may apply for regional locations — please enquire for a quote.

Can we request specific songs? +

Absolutely. We encourage you to share your must-plays, do-not-plays and favourite artists before the day. We build your personalised music plan around these and use our professional judgement to fill the gaps — keeping your dancefloor packed all night.

Do you provide all equipment? +

Yes. We supply premium professional sound systems, lighting equipment, microphones and DJ setup — everything required for a flawless experience. We also carry backup equipment to every event.

How far in advance should we book? +

We recommend booking at least 9–12 months in advance for peak wedding season (October–April). That said, we do sometimes have availability for shorter lead times — it's always worth getting in touch even if your date is soon.

Do you support same-sex and multicultural weddings? +

Wholeheartedly yes. We celebrate every couple, every culture and every kind of love. If your ceremony or reception has specific cultural music traditions, dietary considerations for the run sheet, or any other requirements — just let us know and we'll make it happen.
